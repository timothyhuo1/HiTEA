About Us

About Us

Since 2015, HiTEA Cafe has been synonymous with outstanding boba creations and authentic Asian fare. As we continue to expand our operation, it's been a routine pleasure serving the greater Honolulu area. Our specialization in squeezed juices and scratch-made meals always keep our customers coming back for more. With unique toppings like passion fruit, soft grass jelly, and azuki beans, you can truly customize your dream drink. We're certain from the moment you enjoy a bite of our Minute Chicken, you'll recognize our real passion for all things fresh.




History

In the beginning, we exclusively served bubble tea, smoothies, and fresh-squeezed juice! Now, we've extended our offerings to share the tastiest stir-fried dishes with you. Swing by for a hot and refreshing meal today. Our top priority is to serve the most refreshing drinks with great customer service. We looks forward to meeting you soon!
